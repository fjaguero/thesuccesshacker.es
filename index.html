The Success Hacker A book by Fernando Agüero

A practical guide on productivity, building efficient teams and designing products.

The goal of The Success Hacker is to help people become a better and more positive version of themselves. It's a short compendium of tips on how to acquire good habits, build efficient teams and launch products.

It has nothing to do with ninja tricks or magic beans. It requires taking action to make a change. In line with the book's practical nature, it includes a list of takeaways at the end of each chapter to help you go straight to the point.
